专题一：新高考作文评分标准

评分体系Understanding

新高考英语作文（2024改革版）采用两分法评分体系：应用文（满分15分）和读后续写/概要写作（满分25分），总分40分，占全卷26.7%。两类作文的评分维度有共通之处，但侧重各有不同。

新高考英语作文评分五大维度（雷达图）

一、应用文评分标准（15分）

档次分数内容要求语言要求
第五档（优秀）13-15要点齐全、内容充实、切题语言准确、词汇丰富、句式多样
第四档（良好）10-12要点基本齐全、内容较充实少量语法/词汇错误但不影响理解
第三档（中等）7-9要点有遗漏、内容较单薄错误较多但基本可读
第二档（较差）4-6要点不全、内容偏离错误多、理解困难
第一档（差）1-3要点严重缺失只有零散词汇、无法成文

二、读后续写评分标准（25分）

读后续写从内容情节（10分）、语言表达（10分）、篇章结构（5分）三个维度评分。内容与原文的连贯性是核心评分要素，续写内容必须与原文在情节、人物性格、语言风格上保持高度一致。

高分公式：应用文 = 扣题 + 完整要点 + 得体语言 + 合理结构
读后续写 = 原文连贯 + 情节合理 + 语言丰富 + 风格一致
【示例】2024年新课标I卷应用文满分范文的评分分析
题目：给外教写一封邮件，邀请他担任英语演讲比赛的评委。

满分要素分析：
1. 内容（满分）：时间、地点、邀请原因、比赛详情四项要点完整
2. 语言（满分）：使用"I would be honored if you could...","Looking forward to your favorable reply"等得体表达
3. 结构（满分）：问候→邀请→详情→期待回复，层次清晰
得分拆解：内容5分（满分5）+ 语言5分（满分5）+ 结构5分（满分5）= 15分。关键在于：①所有要点无遗漏；②语言正式得体（适合外教身份）；③段落划分合理，连接词自然。
• 请写出应用文各档次的分数范围及核心区别。

专题二：应用文写作模板

书信通知演讲稿报道

应用文是高考英语写作的第一道考题（15分），要求考生根据情境撰写特定体裁的短文。近五年新高考考查频率显示：书信类占比最高（约58%），其次为演讲稿（18%）和通知（12%）。

新高考应用文体裁考查频率分布图

一、书信/邮件模板（最高频）

书信黄金结构
问候(Dear ...) + 写信目的(I'm writing to ...) + 具体内容 + 期待回复(Looking forward to ...) + 署名(Yours, Li Hua)

六大书信类模板：

【邀请信】
Dear ____,

How is everything going? I'm writing to invite you to ____（活动名称）.

The event will be held at ____（地点）on ____（时间）. We have prepared ____（具体安排）. I'm sure you will enjoy it because ____（邀请理由）. By the way, you don't need to bring anything — your presence is the best gift.

I would be honored if you could come. Looking forward to your reply.

Yours,
Li Hua
【建议信】
Dear ____,

I'm sorry to hear that you ____（问题）. As your friend, I'd like to offer some suggestions.

First of all, it might be a good idea to ____（建议1）. This will help you ____（理由）. Besides, why not ____（建议2）? What's more, I suggest that you ____（建议3）.

I hope my suggestions will be of some help to you. Please let me know if you need further assistance.

Yours,
Li Hua
【申请信】
Dear Sir/Madam,

I'm writing to apply for the position of ____.

I'm a senior student from ____（学校）. I firmly believe I'm qualified for this position for the following reasons. First, ____（优势1）. Second, ____（优势2）. Last but not least, ____（优势3）.

I would appreciate it if you could give me a chance. I'm looking forward to your early reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
【感谢信】
Dear ____,

I'm writing to express my sincere gratitude for ____.

Without your help, I wouldn't have ____（所获帮助的结果）. Your kind words and encouragement meant so much to me. I truly appreciate ____（特别感谢的方面）.

I will always remember your kindness. If there's anything I can do to return the favor, please don't hesitate to let me know.

Yours,
Li Hua
【道歉信】
Dear ____,

I'm terribly sorry to tell you that I cannot ____（道歉事由）. I know this may cause you inconvenience, but I have no choice.

The reason is that ____（原因）. I wonder if it's possible to ____（补救方案）. If not, please let me know and I will try to work out another solution.

Once again, I apologize for any trouble caused. I hope you can understand my situation.

Yours,
Li Hua
【求助信】
Dear ____,

I'm writing to ask for your help. I've been ____（面临的困难）and I really need your advice.

The problem is that ____（具体困难）. I have tried ____（已尝试的方法）, but it didn't work well. So I'm wondering if you could ____（求助内容）. I would really appreciate it if you could give me some guidance.

I'm looking forward to your valuable advice.

Yours,
Li Hua

二、演讲稿模板

【演讲稿】
Good morning, everyone! It's my great honor to stand here and deliver a speech. Today, I'd like to talk about ____（主题）.

As we all know, ____（背景说明）. ____（引出观点）. First and foremost, ____（论点1）. For instance, ____（例证1）. Moreover, ____（论点2）. Take ____ as an example. ____（例证2）. Last but not least, ____（论点3）.

To sum up, ____（总结观点）. I strongly believe that ____（呼吁号召）. Let's take action now!

That's all. Thank you for your listening!

【通知】
NOTICE

In order to ____（目的）, a ____（活动类型）will be held in ____（地点）at ____（时间）on ____（日期）.

All students are welcome. The activity will last about ____（时长）. During the activity, we will ____（活动内容）. Please bring ____（携带物品）. Everyone is required to ____（要求）.

The Students' Union

【真题演练】2023年新课标I卷：假设你是李华，你的英国朋友Jack来信询问如何学好中文。请给他回信，内容包括：1. 表示理解；2. 提出建议（至少三条）；3. 表达祝愿。
参考范文：

Dear Jack,

I'm glad to hear from you. Learning Chinese can be challenging, but don't worry — I'd like to share some effective methods with you.

First of all, it is a good idea to watch Chinese movies or TV dramas. This will help you improve your listening and learn authentic expressions. Besides, why not find a language partner to practice with? Speaking regularly is the key to fluency. What's more, I suggest that you keep a diary in Chinese. Writing every day will gradually expand your vocabulary and improve your grammar.

Rome was not built in a day. Be patient and keep practicing! I believe you will make great progress. Best of luck!

Yours,
Li Hua
解析：本文符合"理解→建议→祝愿"结构。使用了"first of all/besides/what's more"连接三大建议，结尾引用谚语"Rome was not built in a day"升华。句型变化丰富（建议类：it is a good idea to/why not/I suggest that）。
• 假设你是校学生会主席李华，学校将举办一场中国文化节，请你写一则通知，内容包括：活动时间地点、活动内容（书法展览、传统乐器表演、茶艺体验）、参加方式。词数80左右。

专题三：读后续写（上）——情节构建

核心题型Continuation25分

读后续写是新高考试验区推广的核心写作题型（25分），要求考生在阅读一篇350词以内的短文后，根据给定段落开头语（2个段落首句），续写约150词，构成一篇逻辑连贯、情节完整的故事。

读后续写情节发展曲线：从阅读原文到主题升华

读后续写"四步法"：

读后续写四步法流程图

Step 1：读懂原文——"五W一H"要素法

精读原文，提取关键信息：Who（人物及关系）、When/Where（时间地点）、What（事件起因发展）、Why（动机/冲突）、How（情感变化）。特别注意原文的伏笔（foreshadowing）——这些线索必须在续写中得到照应。

Step 2：构思情节——"冲突→解决→升华"三段式

Paragraph 1（根据给定首句展开）：延续冲突 → 尝试解决 → 初步结果
Paragraph 2（根据给定首句展开）：问题最终解决 → 人物感悟 → 主题升华

要素第一段（约80词）第二段（约70词）
内容重心冲突发展、行动尝试问题解决、情感升华
与原文关系承接原文末句的冲突照应原文伏笔、体现成长
情感线紧张→希望→努力放松→喜悦→感悟
关键技巧细节描写（动作/环境）心理描写+主题升华

Step 3：遣词造句——"Show, don't tell"

读后续写的语言要求"展示而非告知"。对比以下表达：

[Tell] He was very nervous.
这是"告知"，平淡无奇
[Show] His heart pounded against his chest, and his hands trembled as he stepped forward.
这是"展示"——通过心跳、手抖来表现紧张，画面感强

Step 4：检查润色——三查法

一查内容连贯：续写是否与原文逻辑一致、伏笔是否照应。
二查语言质量：时态（一般过去时为主）、主谓一致、拼写。
三查情感升华：结尾是否有一句"感悟金句"点明主题。

【示例】原文：Tom was a shy boy who never dared to speak in public. One day, his teacher asked him to give a speech in front of the whole class. Tom's face turned pale. He wanted to refuse, but something inside him said, "Just try."
给定首句：
Paragraph 1: Tom stood up and walked slowly to the platform.
Paragraph 2: When he finished, the classroom erupted in applause.

参考续写：

Paragraph 1: Tom stood up and walked slowly to the platform. His legs were trembling, and his palms were sweating. As he looked at the sea of faces before him, his mind went blank. He took a deep breath, closed his eyes for a moment, and remembered what his mother had said: "You are braver than you think." Opening his eyes, he began to speak. Although his voice was shaky at first, it grew stronger and clearer with each word.

Paragraph 2: When he finished, the classroom erupted in applause. Tom stood there, stunned. His classmates were cheering, and his teacher was smiling at him with pride. At that moment, he realized that courage was not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it. It was a small step for a speech, but a giant leap for his confidence.
解析：① 动作描写细腻（trembling, sweating, took a deep breath）表现紧张；② 内心独白（mother's words）体现人物成长；③ 第二段结尾使用对比（small step vs giant leap）和名句化用（"courage is not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it"），完成主题升华。全文情感线：紧张→努力→惊喜→感悟。
• 原文：Lily found an old, dusty diary in her grandmother's attic. As she opened it, she discovered a love letter written to her grandmother from a young soldier during the war. Write the two paragraphs based on the given opening sentences.
Paragraph 1: Lily's eyes widened as she read the letter.
Paragraph 2: With the diary in her hands, Lily walked to her grandmother's room.

专题四：读后续写（下）——情感与语言升华

高分技巧Language Art

读后续写的语言层次直接影响得分。高分续写的语言必须具备三个特征：画面感（通过感官描写让读者"看见"）、节奏感（长短句交替制造情绪张力）、思想性（结尾的感悟体现文章的深度）。

一、情感描写的"三层递进"

层次基本版 (Low)进阶版 (Medium)高分版 (High)
开心He was happy.A wide smile spread across his face.His face lit up with pure joy, and his eyes sparkled like stars.
伤心She was sad.Tears rolled down her cheeks.She wept silently, her shoulders trembling, as if her whole world had crumbled.
惊讶He was surprised.His jaw dropped in disbelief.He stood frozen, his eyes wide open, struggling to process what he had just heard.
害怕She was scared.Her heart raced with fear.Cold sweat ran down her back, and she felt as if an icy hand was gripping her heart.
愤怒He was angry.His face turned red with anger.Anger surged through him like a volcano erupting, his fists clenched so tightly that his knuckles turned white.

二、环境描写的"以景衬情"

环境描写可以与人物情感形成"同向映衬"或"反向对比"：

The sun dipped below the horizon, painting the sky in shades of orange and pink. He sighed, feeling both sorrow and hope intertwined.
夕阳西下（景）+ 悲喜交织（情）= 同向映衬，画面感强
The rain poured down heavily, yet his heart was singing with joy — today was the day his dream would come true.
滂沱大雨（景）+ 内心欢唱（情）= 反向对比，张力十足

三、高分结尾"金句"模板

读后续写的结尾句往往是得分关键。以下金句模板可直接套用：

感悟型结尾模板（直接套用）
"Sometimes, the greatest lessons come from the smallest moments."
"It was then that I truly understood the meaning of ____."
"Looking back, I realized that every difficulty was actually a gift in disguise."
"The journey taught me that courage does not come from never falling, but from rising every time we fall."

• 读懂原文伏笔 ✓ • 情节与原文一致 ✓ • 使用感官描写 ✓
• 情感变化合理 ✓ • 环境描写衬托 ✓ • 结尾有升华句 ✓
• 时态统一（一般过去时）✓ • 词汇丰富（不用太多I think）✓
【练习】使用以下情景写一段100词的续写结尾（含环境描写+情感升华）：
一位老人终于找到了失散多年的老友。
参考范文：

The autumn leaves danced in the gentle breeze as the two old men stood face to face. For a long moment, neither spoke — words seemed too small for this moment. Then, slowly, tears welled up in their eyes, and they embraced like they would never let go. Time had changed their faces, but not the bond in their hearts. Looking back, they realized that true friendship never fades — it only waits, quietly, for the right moment to bloom again.
解析：① 环境描写（autumn leaves dancing）营造温馨氛围；② 动作细节（tears welled up, embraced）传递情感；③ 结尾升华（true friendship never fades）点明主题。语言使用隐喻（bloom again）、对比（time changed faces, not bond），词汇丰富。
• 改写以下句子，使用"Show, don't tell"原则：
(1) The classroom was very quiet.
(2) She was very excited about the news.
(3) The old house was scary.

专题五：高分句型与亮点表达

句型升级Advanced提分关键

阅卷老师眼中，句型多样性是区分中等作文和高分作文的核心标志。一篇优秀作文应包含3-5种不同句型，避免全文都是"主谓宾"简单句。

一、倒装句——"最显功力"的句式

常见倒装句型：
• Only in this way can we achieve our goal.（Only+状语置于句首）
• Not only does he speak English fluently, but also he writes well.（Not only置于句首）
• So moved was I that tears rolled down my cheeks.（So+adj置于句首）
• Hardly had I arrived home when it began to rain.（Hardly...when结构）
• Never have I seen such a wonderful performance.（否定词置于句首）

二、强调句——"突出重点"的利器

强调句结构：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 剩余部分

• It is perseverance that makes the difference between success and failure.
• It was because of his encouragement that I finally overcame my fear.
• It was not until I failed that I realized the importance of hard work.

三、非谓语动词——"让句子更紧凑"

分词作状语、不定式表目的：

• Inspired by his words, I decided to try again.（过去分词作状语）
• Looking up at the starry sky, he felt a sense of peace.（现在分词作状语）
• To improve my English, I read aloud for 20 minutes every day.（不定式表目的）
• He stood there, not knowing what to say.（分词作伴随状语）

四、高级从句——"体现语法功底"

从句类型示例说明
非限制性定语从句He passed the exam, which surprised everyone.which指代整个主句
同位语从句The news that our team won excited everyone.that引导的从句说明news的内容
主语从句What matters most is not winning but participating.what引导从句作主语
表语从句That's why I believe practice makes perfect.why/that引导从句作表语
让步状语从句However difficult it may be, I'll never give up.however+adj引导的让步
句型升级公式：一篇作文 = 2个倒装/强调 + 2个从句 + 1个非谓语
适可而止，自然融入，不要为了用而用
【升级练习】将以下简单句改写成复合句：
(1) I work hard. I want to achieve my dream.
(2) He is very kind. Everyone likes him.
(3) We should protect the environment. It is very important.
升级版本：

(1) Only by working hard can I achieve my dream.（倒装句）
(2) He is so kind that everyone likes him.（so...that结构）
(3) It is very important that we should protect the environment.（主语从句）
或者 What is very important is that we should protect the environment.（表语从句）
升级的关键在于"化繁为简"中的"化"——不是堆砌复杂结构，而是把原有的简单意思用更高级的表达呈现。每个句子只做一个升级即可，不要在一句话里堆太多结构。
• 用倒装句改写：I can understand the importance of health only after I got sick.
• 用强调句改写：His encouragement helped me overcome the difficulty.
• 用非谓语改写：Because I was encouraged by my teacher, I decided to take part in the competition.

专题六：话题素材库

话题分类Vocabulary素材积累

高考英语作文的高频话题有明确的规律可循。近5年高考语料统计显示，成长励志、社会热点、校园生活三大类话题占据了超过65%的考题。掌握每个话题的核心词汇和经典句型，能在考场上快速成文。

高考英语作文高频话题分布（近5年）

一、成长励志类（最热话题）

核心词汇：perseverance（毅力）、challenge（挑战）、overcome（克服）、confidence（自信）、self-improvement（自我提升）、step out of comfort zone（走出舒适区）

经典句式：
• "It was the most valuable lesson I have ever learned — failure is not the opposite of success, but a stepping stone to it."
• "Looking back, I realize that every challenge I faced was actually a gift in disguise."
• "This experience taught me that courage is not about never being afraid, but about moving forward despite the fear."

二、社会热点类

核心词汇：volunteer（志愿者）、community service（社区服务）、environmental protection（环保）、garbage classification（垃圾分类）、low-carbon life（低碳生活）、public welfare（公益）

经典句式：
• "Small actions can make a big difference. If everyone does their part, our world will become a better place."
• "It's high time that we took action to protect our environment."
• "The experience of volunteering not only benefits the community but also enriches my own life."

三、科技与生活类

核心词汇：artificial intelligence（人工智能）、online learning（在线学习）、digital device（电子设备）、cyber safety（网络安全）、information explosion（信息爆炸）

经典句式：
• "Technology is a double-edged sword. While it brings convenience, it also poses challenges."
• "Every coin has two sides. We should make the best use of technology while avoiding its potential harm."

四、传统文化类

核心词汇：cultural heritage（文化遗产）、traditional customs（传统习俗）、Chinese calligraphy（中国书法）、folk art（民间艺术）、national identity（民族认同）

经典句式：
• "As a Chinese student, I feel it's our responsibility to preserve and promote our traditional culture."
• "The charm of Chinese traditional culture lies not only in its long history but also in its timeless wisdom."
【话题整合练习】以"How to Lead a Low-Carbon Life"为题，写一篇100词的发言稿开头。
参考范文：

Good morning, everyone! Today, I'd like to share some ideas about how to lead a low-carbon life.

As we all know, our planet is facing serious environmental challenges. It's high time that we took action. So what can we do? First, we can choose to walk or cycle instead of taking cars. Second, we should save electricity by turning off lights when leaving rooms. Last but not least, let's reduce waste by refusing disposable products.

Remember: small changes in our daily life can make a big difference to our planet. Let's start from now on!

Thank you!
结构分析：演讲稿格式正确（问候→引入话题→背景→建议→呼吁→感谢）。使用了"It's high time that..."（虚拟语气）、"As we all know"（常见开头）、"Last but not least"（递进连接词）等高分表达。"Small changes... big difference"结尾有力。
• 用英语回答以下问题（至少80词）：
What is the most important lesson you have learned from a failure? How did it change you?

专题七：写作避坑指南

易错点Common Mistakes

高考阅卷中，一些看似"小"的错误会直接影响得分档次。以下是最常见的扣分陷阱及应对策略。

一、应用文"三不犯"原则

错误类型低分版本高分版本扣分点

二、读后续写"六不犯"原则

① 不偏离原文：续写的故事情节必须与原文合理衔接，不得引入原文未出现的新人物。
② 不改变叙事视角：原文是第一人称就续写第一人称，第三人称就续写第三人称。
③ 不滥用对话：对话太多会让故事显得松散，每段最多1-2句关键对话。
④ 不遗漏给定首句：必须包含两个段落的首句，且首句必须出现在段首（不得修改）。
⑤ 不写超长段落：第一段约80词，第二段约70词，总词数150左右。
⑥ 不写负能量结局：高考续写要求正能量结局（成长、感悟、和解）。

雷区错误示例正确示例
时态混用Yesterday I go to the park.Yesterday I went to the park.
主谓不一致Everyone have their own dream.Everyone has their own dream.
中式英语Let me think a think.Let me think about it.
there be 误用There have many students.There are many students.
情态动词+toHe can to swim well.He can swim well.
比较级叠用She is more smarter.She is much smarter.
考场自查口诀：时态要对、主谓要对、单数复数不能忘、中式英语要预防

【纠错练习】找出下面这段话中的错误并改正（共6处错误）：
"Yesterday, I go to the old people's home with my classmate. There have many old people. I tell storys to them. They was very happy. I think it was a meaning day."
正确答案：

"Yesterday, I went to the old people's home with my classmates. There were many old people. I told stories to them. They were very happy. I think it was a meaningful day."

① go→went（时态）② classmate→classmates（单复数）③ have→were（there be句型）④ storys→stories（拼写）⑤ was→were（主谓一致）⑥ meaning→meaningful（词性）
这6处错误代表了高考作文最常见的错误类型。尤其要注意：there be句型和主谓一致是阅卷老师重点关注的基本功。

(1) He don't like playing basketball.
(2) She studies very hardly.
(3) There is two books on the desk.

专题八：考场策略与时间分配

策略Strategy时间管理

高考英语写作部分（应用文+读后续写）建议分配35-40分钟。合理的时间规划比盲目动笔更重要。

推荐时间分配表：

阶段应用文（15分）读后续写（25分）总计
审题构思3 min5 min8 min
写草稿8 min12 min20 min
检查修改2 min5 min7 min
总计13 min22 min35 min

应用文"3-8-2"策略：

3分钟审题：圈出情境、体裁、要点（不少于三个要点）、人称、时态。
8分钟写作：先写框架（每段首句），再填充内容。使用连接词确保层次清晰。
2分钟检查：①格式（称呼/署名）；②要点是否齐全；③拼写和语法。

读后续写"5-12-5"策略：

5分钟精读原文：圈出人物、冲突、伏笔、情感基调。读两遍！！！
12分钟写作：先写第二段结尾（确保终点明确），再倒回来写第一段。
5分钟检查：①情节是否与原文连贯；②首句是否自然衔接；③语言质量（时态/主谓一致/词汇）；④有无提升金句。

考场锦囊
• 字迹工整 = 隐形加2分——阅卷老师每天批改数百份，字迹清楚直接提升印象分
• 宁可简单不出错，不要炫技出硬伤——没有把握的复杂句不如不用
• 应用文不出彩也要保底——格式正确、要点齐全就已进入二档文（10-12分）
• 读后续写必须写完——哪怕最后一句简单收尾，也比写到一半强得多
【综合练习】限时模拟——在30分钟内完成以下两道题：
(1) 应用文：假设你是李华，你的学校将举办"中国文化节"，请你写一封邮件邀请你的外教Mr. Smith参加。（15分）
(2) 读后续写：阅读下面短文，根据所给两段首句续写。（25分）
应用文参考范文：

Dear Mr. Smith,

I'm writing to invite you to our school's Chinese Culture Festival, which will be held on Friday, June 16th, from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. in the school hall.

Various activities have been prepared, including traditional calligraphy exhibitions, folk music performances, and tea ceremony experiences. I believe it will be a wonderful opportunity for you to experience Chinese culture firsthand. You might even try your hand at calligraphy!

We would be honored to have you with us. Looking forward to your reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Hua
范文分析：① 格式完整（称呼、正文、结束语、署名）；② 要点齐全（活动名称、时间地点、活动内容、邀请意图）；③ 语言得体（I would be honored...）；④ 结构清晰（写信目的→详情→邀请→期待）。建议在使用前先自行思考，再看参考范文进行比较。
