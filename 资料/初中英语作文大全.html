一、作文评分标准（中考）
🔹 评分维度
维度满分说明
内容6分要点齐全、内容充实、主题明确
语言6分语法正确、词汇丰富、句式多样
结构4分条理清晰、衔接自然、逻辑连贯
书写2分卷面整洁、字迹工整
二类文（12-14分） 要点齐全，有少量语法/拼写错误
一类文（15-18分） 内容充实，语言准确，结构清晰，句式多样
各档要求
① 扣题不漏点（内容分拿满）
② 语法少犯错（语言分拿满）
③ 分段+连接词（结构分拿满）
④ 字迹工整（书写分拿满）
一类文 = 内容充实 + 语言准确 + 结构清晰 + 字迹工整
二、常见写作体裁
🔹 记叙文
特点 记述人物经历或事件发展过程
结构 开头（背景介绍）→ 经过（发展/高潮）→ 结尾（感受/感悟）
常用词汇
· 时间顺序：one day, first, then, after that, later, finally, at last, in the end
· 情感词汇：happy, excited, moved, surprised, sad, worried, proud
· 连接词：when, while, as, before, after, as soon as, suddenly
写作要点
① 六要素齐全：时间(when)、地点(where)、人物(who)、起因(why)、经过(what)、结果(how)
② 使用过去时（一般过去时为主）
③ 详略得当，重点描写高潮部分
④ 结尾要有感悟或总结
常见命题 An Unforgettable Day / My Best Friend / A Trip to... / My School Life
记叙文 = 背景 + 经过 + 感悟 | 一般过去时 | 六要素齐全
🔹 说明文
特点 介绍事物/人物/地方/方法
结构 引入（说明对象）→ 主体（特征/功能/方法）→ 结尾（总结/评价）
常用句型
· 介绍：... is a ... / There be ... / It is located in ... / ... is known for ...
· 特征：It has ... / It is made of ... / It looks like ...
· 用途：... is used for ... / ... can be used to ...
· 方法：First, ... / Second, ... / Then, ... / Finally, ...
常用词汇
· 空间顺序：on the left/right, next to, in front of, behind, between, in the middle
· 分类：kind, type, sort, category
常见命题 My Hometown / My Room / How to Learn English Well / My Favorite Subject
说明文 = 对象 + 特征/方法 + 总结 | 一般现在时 | 空间/逻辑顺序
🔹 议论文
特点 就某观点发表看法并论证
结构 引出话题 → 表明观点 → 论证（2-3个理由）→ 结论/号召
常用句型
· 引出话题：Different people have different opinions. / Recently, ... has become a hot topic.
· 表明观点：In my opinion, ... / As far as I'm concerned, ... / I believe that ...
· 列举理由：First of all, ... / What's more, ... / Last but not least, ...
· 举例：For example, ... / For instance, ...
· 让步：Although/Though ..., ... / It is true that ..., but ...
· 总结：In a word, ... / All in all, ... / In short, ...
常用词汇
· 因果：because, since, therefore, as a result, thanks to
· 递进：besides, moreover, furthermore, what's more
· 转折：but, however, on the other hand, while
常见命题 Should Students Use Mobile Phones? / Is Homework Necessary? / My View on Online Learning
议论文 = 观点 + 理由(2-3个) + 结论 | 连接词丰富 | 论证充分
🔹 应用文
书信/邮件
格式：称呼(Dear ...,) → 正文 → 结束语(Best wishes./Yours,) → 署名
常用句型
· 开头：I'm writing to ... / How is everything going? / I'm glad to hear from you.
· 正文：I'd like to ... / Could you please ...? / I'm looking forward to ...
· 结尾：I hope to hear from you soon. / Looking forward to your reply.
通知
格式：标题(NOTICE) → 正文（时间/地点/活动/要求）→ 发布单位和日期
常用句型
· May I have your attention, please? / There will be a ... / Everyone is required to ...
· Please be on time. / Don't forget to ... / All are welcome.
日记
格式：日期 + 星期 + 天气 → 正文
常用词汇 sunny, cloudy, rainy, windy, warm, cold
· 今天发生/做了某事：Today I ... / This morning I ...
发言稿/演讲稿
· 开头：Good morning, everyone. / It's my honor to be here to ...
· 正文：Today I'd like to talk about ... / As we all know ...
· 结尾：That's all. Thank you for listening. / Thank you!
书信：称呼+正文+结束语+署名 | 通知：标题+正文+发布单位 | 日记：日期+天气+正文

议论文
· Different people have different opinions on whether students should use mobile phones.

As we all know / There is no doubt that / Nowadays / I'm writing to...
🔹 正文句型
理由/观点
· First of all, we can learn a lot from reading. Besides, reading broadens our mind. Last but not least, reading is a great way to relax.
· There are several reasons for this. First, ... Second, ... Third, ...

举例
· For example, my best friend Tom is a good student.
· Take my brother as an example. He ...
· Such as playing basketball, swimming and reading.
比较对比
· Compared with traditional teaching, online learning is more convenient.
· Just as a coin has two sides, every coin has two sides.
· While some students like studying alone, others prefer group study.
· A is more important than B. / A is as important as B.
补充/递进
· What's more, the environment there is very beautiful.
· Besides, we can also learn team spirit through sports.
· Moreover, the cost of living is not very high.
转折
· However, everything has its disadvantages.
· On the contrary, I think we should try our best.
· Although it is difficult, I will never give up.
🔹 结尾句型
总结观点
· In a word, books are our best friends.
· In short, health is more important than wealth.
· All in all, we should try our best to protect the environment.
· From what has been discussed above, we may safely draw the conclusion that ...
表达愿望/建议
· I hope that my dream will come true one day.
· I strongly suggest that everyone should take part in sports.
· Only in this way can we achieve our goals.（倒装句）
· Let's take action to protect our earth.
书信类结尾
· I'm looking forward to your early reply.
· I would be grateful if you could join us.
· Best wishes to you!
· Write to me soon.
In a word / All in all / I hope that / Looking forward to | 结尾扣题+升华
🔹 高分亮点句型
（使用以下句型能让作文提升一个档次）

定语从句
· I have a friend who is very good at English.
· I will never forget the day when I first met him.
· This is the best movie that I have ever seen.
宾语从句
· I believe that we can make our city more beautiful.
· We all know that practice makes perfect.
状语从句
· If we work hard, we will succeed.
· I will never give up even though I fail many times.
· When I grow up, I want to be a teacher.
非谓语动词
· To improve my English, I read English every day.（不定式表目的）
· The boy sitting under the tree is my brother.（现在分词作定语）
· Compared with other subjects, I like English best.（过去分词作状语）
倒装句
· Only in this way can we learn English well.
· Not only does he study hard, but also he helps others.
· So beautiful is the city that many people visit it every year.
it句型
· It is important for us to learn English well.
· It is known to all that the earth moves around the sun.
· It is said that the sports meeting will be put off.
亮点句型：定从/宾从/状从/非谓/倒装/it句型 | 每篇用2-3个亮点句即可
四、作文模板
🔹 记叙文模板
An Unforgettable Experience

Everyone has some unforgettable experiences. So do I.

It happened on a sunny morning last summer. I went to the old people's home with my classmates. When we arrived, we cleaned the rooms and talked with the old people. I told them some interesting stories and they laughed happily. What touched me most was that an old woman held my hand and said, "Thank you, my child."

From this experience, I have learned that we should care for the old and help those in need. Helping others makes me happy. I will never forget this meaningful day.
模板结构
开头（总起句：人人都有难忘经历）→ 时间+地点+人物+事件经过（过去时）→ 最感动的细节 → 结尾感悟（学会的道理）
🔹 议论文模板
My View on Using Mobile Phones at School

Nowadays, more and more students have mobile phones. Different people have different opinions on whether students should use them at school.

In my opinion, students should not use mobile phones at school. First of all, using phones in class distracts us from our studies. What's more, playing phone games too much is bad for our eyes. Last but not least, some students compare their phones with each other, which is not a good habit.

However, phones can be useful in some ways. We can use them to look up new words or search for information. But we should use them properly.

All in all, I think we should not use mobile phones at school. Let's focus on our studies and use phones only when necessary.
模板结构
引出话题（不同人有不同看法）→ 表明观点 → 列举理由(First/What's more/Last but not least) → 让步(However) → 总结(All in all)
🔹 说明文模板
My Best Friend

I have a good friend. Her name is Li Hua. We have been friends for three years.

She is a tall and thin girl with short hair. She has big eyes and a round face, and she always wears a smile. She is kind-hearted and hard-working. When I have difficulties in study, she is always ready to help me. She is good at English and math. Besides, she is interested in reading and swimming.

I like my best friend very much. I hope our friendship will last forever.
模板结构
引入人物 → 外貌特征 → 性格特点 → 具体事例 → 总结感受
🔹 书信模板
Dear Tom,

How is everything going? I'm writing to invite you to my birthday party.

The party will be held at 6:00 p.m. on Saturday, May 20th, at my home. My parents will prepare a lot of delicious food. After dinner, we will sing songs, play games and watch a movie. I hope you can come. By the way, you don't need to bring any gifts. Your coming is the best gift for me.

Would you like to come? I'm looking forward to your reply.

Yours,
Li Hua
模板结构
问候 → 写信目的 → 具体信息(时间/地点/活动) → 补充提醒 → 期待回复 → 署名
🔹 发言稿模板
Good morning, everyone! It's my great honor to stand here and give a speech. Today, I'd like to talk about how to protect our environment.

As we all know, the environment is getting worse and worse. It is our duty to protect it. First, we should not litter anywhere. Second, we'd better go to school by bike or on foot instead of by car. Third, we should save water and electricity in our daily life. Also, we can plant more trees to make our city greener.

Let's take action now! Small actions can make a big difference. If everyone does something, our world will become a better place.

That's all. Thank you for listening!
模板结构
问候+引入话题 → 背景说明 → 逐条建议(First/Second/Third) → 呼吁号召 → 感谢听众

六、常见写作错误与避坑
🔹 十大常见错误
错误类型错误正确
主谓一致He like music.He likes music.
时态错误Yesterday I go to school.Yesterday I went to school.
名词单复数I have two book.I have two books.
形容词性物主代词This is I book.This is my book.
There beThere have a book.There is a book.
情态动词后He can to swim.He can swim.
比较级He is more tall than me.He is taller than me.
动词+ingI enjoy read.I enjoy reading.
及物/不及物I discussed about it.I discussed it.
拼写错误recieve / becuasereceive / because
检查方法 写完后逐句检查：每个动词的时态/语态/主谓一致是否正确
常见错误：主谓一致/时态/单复数/形代/T Here be/情态后动原 | 写完后逐句检查
