📝 初中英语 · 语法大全

词性 · 时态 · 语态 · 从句 · 非谓语 · 中考必备

一、词性分类（Parts of Speech）
🔹 名词
可数名词 有单复数之分：
规则复数 一般+s (book→books) | 以s/x/sh/ch结尾+es (box→boxes) | 辅音+y变y为i+es (city→cities) | 以o结尾：有生命+es (potato→potatoes)，无生命+s (photo→photos) | 以f/fe结尾变f/fe为v+es (knife→knives)
不规则复数 man→men, woman→women, child→children, foot→feet, tooth→teeth, mouse→mice, sheep→sheep, deer→deer, Chinese→Chinese, phenomenon→phenomena
不可数名词 无复数形式，不能与a/an连用：
液体 water, milk, tea, coffee, juice, oil
气体 air, oxygen, smoke
食物 bread, rice, meat, beef, chicken, fish, fruit, sugar, salt
抽象 information, news, advice, knowledge, fun, weather, music, time, homework, housework
不可数量化 a piece of news, a glass of water, a cup of tea, a bag of rice
名词所有格
's 所有格 有生命名词：Tom's book, the students' classroom, children's day, my sister's friend
of 所有格 无生命名词：the name of the school, the color of the sky, the door of the room
双重所有格 a friend of my father's, a book of Tom's
可数：单数→复数（规则+s/es） | 不可数：无复数，用量词修饰
🔹 冠词
不定冠词 a/an 表示"一个/某个"：a用于辅音音素前 (a book, a university, a one-hour trip)，an用于元音音素前 (an apple, an hour, an honest boy, an NBA game)
定冠词 the 表示"这个/那个"：
① 上文提到过的：I have a cat. The cat is black.
② 谈话双方都知道的：Please close the door.
③ 独一无二的：the sun, the moon, the earth
④ 序数词/最高级前：the first, the best
⑤ 乐器前：play the piano/guitar/violin
⑥ 姓氏复数前表示一家人：the Greens
⑦ 形容词前表示一类人：the rich, the poor, the young
零冠词 不用冠词的情况：
① 球类/棋类前：play basketball/chess
② 三餐前：have breakfast/lunch
③ 学科前：study English/math
④ 专有名词前：China, Beijing, Tom
⑤ 固定短语：at home, go to school, by bus, in bed, at night
a + 辅音音素 | an + 元音音素 | the = 特指 | 零冠词：球/餐/学科/专名
🔹 代词
人称代词
数单数复数
主格宾格主格宾格
第一人称Imeweus
第二人称youyouyouyou
第三人称he/she/ithim/her/ittheythem
物主代词
类型myyourhisheritsouryourtheir
形容词性myyourhisheritsouryourtheir
名词性mineyourshishersitsoursyourstheirs
记忆口诀 形容词性物主代词后必接名词，名词性物主代词独立使用（This is my book. = This book is mine.）
反身代词 myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves
常见短语：enjoy oneself（玩得开心）, by oneself（独自）, teach oneself（自学）, help oneself to（随便吃）
指示代词 this(这个)/that(那个) / these(这些)/those(那些)
不定代词 some(用于肯定)/any(用于否定疑问) | many(可数)/much(不可数) | (a)few(可数)/(a)little(不可数) | both(两者都)/all(三者都) | either(两者任一)/neither(两者都不)/none(三者都不) | each(每个)/every(每一个) | other(另一个)/another(再一个) | something/anything/nothing/everything
疑问代词 who(谁主格), whom(谁宾格), whose(谁的), which(哪个), what(什么)
形代+名 = 名代 | some/any | many/much | few/little | both/all | either/neither/none
🔹 数词
基数词 表示数量：one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, eleven, twelve, thirteen, fourteen, fifteen, sixteen, seventeen, eighteen, nineteen, twenty, twenty-one..., thirty, forty, fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty, ninety, one hundred, one thousand, one million
注意 thirteen(13), fifteen(15), eighteen(18) 拼写特殊 | twenty(20), thirty(30), forty(40), fifty(50), eighty(80) 拼写特殊
序数词 表示顺序：first, second, third, fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, eighth, ninth, tenth, eleventh, twelfth, thirteenth, fourteenth, fifteenth, sixteenth, seventeenth, eighteenth, nineteenth, twentieth, twenty-first..., thirtieth, fortieth, fiftieth, sixtieth, seventieth, eightieth, ninetieth, hundredth
口诀 一二三：first, second, third；八减t九去e，ve变f（five→fifth, twelve→twelfth）；ty变tie再加th（twenty→twentieth）
用法 ① 序数词前一般加the（the first lesson）② 编号用基数词：Lesson One, Page 10, Room 301, Bus No.5 ③ 年月日：on May 1st（读作on May the first） ④ 分数：分子用基数分母用序数（分子>1分母+s）：1/3→one third, 2/5→two fifths
基数：数量 | 序数：顺序（the + first/second...）| 分数：分子基+分母序（>1+s）
🔹 介词
时间介词 at + 时间点/年龄：at 7:00, at noon, at night, at the age of 12
in + 年/月/季节/一天中的部分：in 2024, in May, in summer, in the morning, in the future
on + 具体某天/星期/节日/日期：on Monday, on May 1st, on Sunday morning, on a cold night
for + 时间段（完成时常用）：for two hours, for three years
since + 时间点（完成时常用）：since 2020, since last week
by 到...为止 | until 直到 | during 在...期间 | before/after 在...前/后
地点介词 at 小地点：at school, at home, at the bus stop, at the door
in 大地点/内部：in Beijing, in the room, in the box, in the street（英式）
on 在...表面/在（路）：on the desk, on the wall, on the left, on the street（美式）
under 在...下 | behind 在...后 | in front of 在...前(外部) | in the front of 在...前(内部)
between 两者之间 | among 三者以上之间 | next to 紧挨着 | across from 在...对面
across 横穿（表面）| through 穿过（内部）| over 在...上方(正上方) | above 在...上方(不一定正)
方式介词 by + 交通工具：by bus/train/plane/bike/car/ship | by + doing：by working hard
in + 语言/材料：in English, in Chinese, in ink
with + 工具/伴随：write with a pen, with Tom, with pleasure
时间：at+点·in+月年·on+天 | 交通：by+工具（无冠词） | 穿行：across表面·through内部
🔹 连词
并列连词 连接并列的词/短语/从句：
and 和，并且：Tom and I, work hard and succeed
or 或者/否则：tea or coffee? Hurry up, or you'll be late.
but 但是：I like math, but I don't like physics.
so 所以（因果）：It rained, so we stayed at home.
注意 because和so不能连用 | although/though和but不能连用
从属连词 引导从句：
when/while/as 当...时 | before 在...前 | after 在...后 | until 直到
if/unless 如果/除非（条件） | because 因为 | since 既然/自从
although/though 虽然 | so that 以便 | so...that 如此...以至于
as...as 和...一样 | than 比
and(和) or(或/否则) but(但是) so(所以) | because≠so | although≠but
🔹 形容词
用法 ① 作定语修饰名词：a beautiful girl ② 作表语放在系动词后：She is beautiful. ③ 作宾补：Make the world beautiful.
比较级和最高级构成
① 单音节/部分双音节：+er/est（tall→taller→tallest, late→later→latest, big→bigger→biggest, easy→easier→easiest）
② 多音节/部分双音节：more/most + 原级（beautiful→more beautiful→most beautiful）
③ 不规则：good/well→better→best, bad/badly→worse→worst, many/much→more→most, little→less→least, far→farther/further→farthest/furthest, old→older/elder→oldest/eldest
重要句型
① A = B：A is as + 原级 + as B（Tom is as tall as Mike.）
② A ≠ B：A is not as/so + 原级 + as B（Tom is not as tall as Mike.）
③ 比较：A is 比较级 + than B（Tom is taller than Mike.）
④ 最高级：A is the + 最高级 + 范围（Tom is the tallest in our class.）
⑤ 越来越：比较级 + and + 比较级（The weather is getting colder and colder.）
⑥ 越...越...：the + 比较级, the + 比较级（The more you read, the more you learn.）
修饰比较级 much, a lot, far, even, still, a little, a bit（much better, a little bigger）
原级：as+原+as | 比较级：比较级+than | 最高级：the+最高+范围
🔹 副词
分类
时间 now, then, today, yesterday, tomorrow, soon, already, yet, ever, never, always, usually, often, sometimes, seldom, hardly
地点 here, there, home, upstairs, downstairs, everywhere, inside, outside, away
方式 slowly, quickly, carefully, happily, loudly, suddenly, well, hard
程度 very, quite, too, so, enough, almost, nearly, hardly, just
频率 always(100%), usually(80%), often(60%), sometimes(40%), seldom(20%), never(0%)
位置
① 频度副词：放在be动词/助动词/情态动词之后，实义动词之前（I never eat junk food. / He is always late.）
② 程度副词：放在被修饰词前（very good, quite well）
③ 方式副词：放在动词后（She sings well.）
④ 时间/地点副词：放在句首或句末（Yesterday I met him. / I met him yesterday.）
比较级/最高级 规则同形容词（fast→faster→fastest, carefully→more carefully→most carefully, well→better→best, badly→worse→worst）
频度：be后/助后/情后/实前 | 程度：被修饰词前 | 方式：动词后
🔹 动词
行为动词 表示动作或状态：及物动词（后接宾语：eat, like, buy, make）| 不及物动词（不接宾语：run, sleep, arrive, go）
连系动词 后接表语：
① be动词（am/is/are/was/were）
② 感官系动词：look（看起来）, sound（听起来）, smell（闻起来）, taste（尝起来）, feel（摸起来）
③ 变化系动词：become, get, turn, grow
④ 保持系动词：keep, stay, remain
助动词 帮助构成时态/语态/否定/疑问：
be 构成进行时/被动语态 | do/does/did 构成否定/疑问 | have/has/had 构成完成时 | will/would/shall 构成将来时
情态动词 表示可能性/必要性/意愿，后接动词原形：
can/could 能/可以（could更委婉）→ be able to（替代将来/完成时）
may/might 可能/可以（might更委婉）
must 必须（主观）→ have to（客观, 有时态变化）
mustn't 禁止 | needn't = don't have to 不必
should 应该 | had better 最好（+ do / not do）
would like to 想要 | used to 过去常常（+ do）
情态动词表推测 must(一定), can't(不可能), may/might(可能)：He must be at home.（他一定在家。）/ He can't be at home.（他不可能在家。）/ He may be at home.（他可能在家。）
系动词：be/感官/变化/保持 | 情态：后+动原 | 推测：must一定·can't不可能·may可能
二、动词时态（Tenses）
🔹 一般现在时
结构
① be动词：I + am / 单数主语 + is / 复数主语 + are
② 实义动词：主语(非三单) + 动词原形 / 主语(三单) + 动词-s/es
否定 am/is/are + not | don't/doesn't + 动原 | 疑问 Be提前 | Do/Does + 主语 + 动原
动词三单变化规则 一般+s（like→likes）| 以s/x/sh/ch/o结尾+es（go→goes, watch→watches）| 辅音+y变y为i+es（study→studies）| have→has
用法
① 经常/习惯性动作：I get up at 6 every day.
② 客观真理/科学事实：The sun rises in the east.
③ 主语的特征/状态：He likes music.
④ 时刻表/计划（用一般现在表将来）：The train leaves at 8.
标志词 often, usually, always, sometimes, seldom, never, every day/week, once a week, on Sundays
主语(三单)+动词-s/es | 主语(非三单)+动原 | 标志：often/usually/every day
🔹 一般过去时
结构
① be动词：was（I/单数）/ were（复数）
② 实义动词：主语 + 动词过去式
否定 was/were + not | didn't + 动原 | 疑问 Was/Were提前 | Did + 主语 + 动原
动词过去式变化
规则：一般+ed（work→worked）| 以e结尾+d（live→lived）| 辅音+y变y为i+ed（study→studied）| 重读闭音节双写+ed（stop→stopped, plan→planned）
不规则常用表 am/is→was, are→were, go→went, have/has→had, do→did, get→got, eat→ate, see→saw, come→came, take→took, make→made, buy→bought, think→thought, teach→taught, tell→told, speak→spoke, write→wrote, read→read, put→put, cut→cut, pay→paid, say→said, spend→spent, build→built, feel→felt, sleep→slept, meet→met, leave→left, lose→lost, win→won
用法
① 过去发生的动作/状态：I bought a book yesterday.
② 过去习惯/反复动作：He often played basketball after school last year.
标志词 yesterday, last night/week/year, ...ago, in 2020, just now, the day before yesterday, once upon a time
主语+动词过去式 | 不规则动词需特殊记忆 | 标志：yesterday/last/...ago
🔹 一般将来时
结构一：will/shall + 动词原形
will用于所有人称，shall用于第一人称(I/we)：I will go. / We shall go.
否定：will not = won't | 疑问：Will + 主语 + 动原
用法 ① 主观意愿：I will help you. ② 预测：It will rain tomorrow.
结构二：be going to + 动词原形
am/is/are going to + 动原
用法 ① 计划打算：I am going to visit Beijing. ② 有迹象要发生：Look at the clouds! It is going to rain.
区别 will侧重主观意愿/临时决定，be going to侧重计划/有迹象
其他表达将来
① 现在进行时表将来（go/come/leave/arrive/start等位移动词）：I am leaving tomorrow.
② 一般现在时表将来（时刻表）：The train leaves at 8.
标志词 tomorrow, next week/month, soon, in the future, in two days, this evening, this weekend
will + 动原（意愿/预测）| be going to + 动原（计划/迹象）
🔹 现在进行时
结构 am/is/are + doing（现在分词）
否定：am/is/are + not + doing | 疑问：Be + 主语 + doing
现在分词变化 一般+ing（doing, playing）| 以不发音e结尾去e+ing（making, writing）| 重读闭音节双写+ing（running, swimming, getting, sitting, beginning）| ie变y+ing（die→dying, lie→lying, tie→tying）
用法
① 此刻正在进行的动作：I am reading a book now.
② 当前阶段正在做（不一定此刻）：He is writing a novel these days.
③ 表示变化/趋势：The weather is getting warmer.
④ 位移动词表将来：I am coming!
标志词 now, at present, at the moment, Look!, Listen!, these days
不用于进行时的动词 ① 状态动词：know, believe, understand, belong ② 感官动词：see, hear, smell, taste ③ 情感动词：like, love, hate, want, wish（但可以用：I'm loving it. 口语）
am/is/are + doing | 此刻正在发生 | Listen!/Look! + 现在进行时
🔹 过去进行时
结构 was/were + doing
否定：was/were + not + doing | 疑问：Was/Were + 主语 + doing
用法
① 过去某一时刻正在发生的动作：I was watching TV at 8 last night.
② 过去某段时间正在进行的动作：They were building a house last summer.
③ 一个动作发生时另一个动作正在进行（用while连接）：While I was sleeping, someone knocked at the door.
when vs while
when + 短暂性动词/持续性动词：I was reading when he came in.
while + 持续性动词/进行时：While I was reading, he came in.
标志词 at 8 last night, at this time yesterday, at that time, when/while引导的时间状语
was/were + doing | 过去某刻正在进行 | when+瞬间/while+持续
🔹 现在完成时
结构 have/has + done（过去分词）
否定：have/has + not + done | 疑问：Have/Has + 主语 + done
过去分词 规则同过去式+ed | 不规则需单独记忆（done, gone, seen, eaten, bought, taught, told, written, spoken, read, put, cut, known, grown, thrown, drawn, broken, chosen, driven, ridden, sung, drunk, swum, begun, run, come, become, flown）
用法
① 过去的动作对现在有影响/结果：I have lost my key.（现在找不到了）
② 过去的动作持续到现在（可能继续）：He has lived here for 10 years.
③ 经历/经验：I have been to Beijing.（去过北京）
与一般过去时区别 一般过去强调过去发生的动作，现在完成强调对现在的影响
标志词 already(肯定), yet(否定/疑问), just, ever, never, recently/lately, so far, up to now, in the past/last few years, since + 时间点, for + 时间段
短暂性动词 在完成时中不能与for/since连用，需转换为：
buy → have had | borrow → have kept | leave → have been away | die → have been dead | join → have been in/a member of | begin → have been on | come/go → have been in
have been to vs have gone to
have been to 去过（已回来）：I have been to Shanghai twice.
have gone to 去了（还没回来）：He has gone to Shanghai.（人不在这儿）
have/has+过去分词 | already(肯)/yet(否疑) | been to(去过)/gone to(去了)
🔹 过去完成时
结构 had + done（过去分词）
否定：had not + done = hadn't done | 疑问：Had + 主语 + done
用法
① 表示过去某一时间之前已经完成的动作（"过去的过去"）：When I got to the station, the train had already left.
② 表示过去某一时间持续到过去另一时间的动作：He had taught at this school for 20 years by 2020.
标志词 by/before + 过去时间, by the time, when/before/after/until + 从句（动作在前用过去完成）, already（在过去之前）
had + 过去分词 | 过去的过去 | by/before + 过去时间
🔹 过去将来时
结构
① would + 动词原形
② was/were going to + 动词原形
用法
从过去某一时间看将要发生的动作或状态，常用于宾语从句（主句过去时，从句将来时用过去将来）：
He said he would come the next day.
I didn't know if she was going to attend the meeting.
They told me they would not give up.
would + 动原 | was/were going to + 动原 | 常用于宾语从句
🔹 时态对比总结
时态结构核心用法标志词
一般现在时do/does经常/习惯/真理often/every/day
一般过去时did过去发生yesterday/last/ago
一般将来时will/be going to将要发生tomorrow/next
现在进行时am/is/are + doing现在正在now/Listen/Look
过去进行时was/were + doing过去某刻正在at 8 last night
现在完成时have/has + done过去对现在影响already/yet/since/for
过去完成时had + done过去的过去by/before + 过去
过去将来时would + do从过去看将来said/told + would
三、被动语态（Passive Voice）
🔹 被动语态结构
基本结构：be + done（过去分词）
时态主动被动
一般现在时cleanam/is/are + cleaned
一般过去时cleanedwas/were + cleaned
一般将来时will cleanwill be + cleaned
现在进行时am/is/are cleaningam/is/are being + cleaned
过去进行时was/were cleaningwas/were being + cleaned
现在完成时have/has cleanedhave/has been + cleaned
情态动词can cleancan be + cleaned
用法
① 不知道动作执行者：My bike was stolen last night.
② 强调动作承受者：English is spoken all over the world.
③ 动作执行者不言自明：He was born in 2010.
by短语 如需提及动作执行者，用by：The cake was made by my mother.
主动变被动：宾语变主语+be+过去分词(+by原主语) | 时态体现在be上
🔹 被动语态特殊情况
双宾语 间接宾语+直接宾语 → 两种被动：
I gave him a book. → He was given a book. / A book was given to him.
感官动词/使役动词 see/hear/watch/feel/make/have/let + sb + do → 被动加to：
I saw him cross the street. → He was seen to cross the street.
The boss made them work. → They were made to work.
短语动词 变为被动时保持短语完整：
The nurses look after the patients. → The patients are looked after by the nurses.
主动表被动
① 某些系动词用主动表被动：The book sells well. / The cloth feels soft.
② need/want/require + doing = need + to be done：The car needs washing = The car needs to be washed.
使役/感官动词被动+to | 短语动词保持完整 | need doing = need to be done
四、非谓语动词（Non-finite Verbs）
🔹 动词不定式（to do）
形式 to + 动词原形（否定：not to do）
作主语 To learn English is important. = It is important to learn English.（it作形式主语）
作宾语 常见动词：want, hope, wish, expect, decide, plan, agree, refuse, learn, promise, choose, fail, pretend, manage, would like：I want to go home.
作宾补 常见动词：ask, tell, want, wish, expect, allow, advise, invite, force, encourage, teach, warn, order：He asked me to help him.
作定语 I have something to say. / I need a pen to write with.
作状语 ① 目的：He came to borrow a book. ② 结果：He woke up to find everyone gone. ③ 原因：I'm glad to see you.
不带to的情况
① 使役动词：let/make/have + sb + do（但被动要加to）
② 感官动词：see/hear/watch/feel/notice + sb + do（被动要加to）
③ 情态动词后：can/may/must/should + do
④ had better + do / would rather + do / why not + do
疑问词+不定式 what/when/where/how/which + to do：I don't know what to do. / He showed me how to use it.
to+动原 | 使役/感官动词后省to（主动）| 疑问词+to do = 名词性成分
🔹 动名词（doing）
形式 动词-ing形式，具有名词性质
作主语 Swimming is good for health. / It is no use crying over spilt milk.（it作形式主语）
作宾语 常见后接doing的动词：enjoy, finish, mind, keep, practice, suggest, avoid, consider, admit, deny, imagine, risk, give up, put off, can't help, be worth, be busy, feel like：I enjoy reading books. / Would you mind opening the door?
介词后接动名词 be good at, be interested in, be afraid of, look forward to, be used to, pay attention to, thanks for, instead of, without, by：Thank you for helping me.
"to"是介词的短语 pay attention to, look forward to, be used to, get used to, stick to, lead to, make contributions to（后+doing而非动词原形）：I'm looking forward to hearing from you.
作表语 My hobby is collecting stamps.
固定接doing：enjoy/finish/mind/keep/practice/suggest/avoid | 介词后+doing
🔹 不定式 vs 动名词 对比
to do 和 doing 意义不同的动词
动词+ to do+ doing
forget忘记要去做忘记做过
remember记得要去做记得做过
stop停下来开始做停止做
try尽力做尝试做
go on继续做（另一件）继续做（同一件）
mean打算做意味着
like/love/hate/prefer 后接to do和doing区别不大，但表示具体某一次常用to do：I like swimming. / I like to swim this afternoon.
remember/forget/stop/try + to do(未做) vs + doing(已做)
🔹 分词（现在分词/过去分词）
现在分词（doing） 主动/进行 过去分词（done） 被动/完成
作定语
① 单个分词在前：a sleeping baby = a baby who is sleeping | a broken window = a window that was broken
② 分词短语在后：The boy sitting there is Tom. / The book written by Lu Xun is popular.
作状语
① Seeing the teacher, he said hello.（时间/原因，主动）
② Given more time, I can do it better.（条件，被动）
作宾补
I saw him crossing the street.（正在过）/ I saw him cross the street.（过了）/ I had my hair cut.（让别人做）
现在分词：主动进行 | 过去分词：被动完成 | 作定语/状语/宾补
五、从句（Clauses）
🔹 宾语从句
定义 在句子中充当宾语的从句
连接词
① that 陈述句（可省略）：I think that he is right.
② if/whether 一般疑问句（是否）：I don't know if/whether he will come.
③ 疑问词 特殊疑问句：Can you tell me where he lives?
三要素
① 连接词（如上）
② 语序 宾语从句必须用陈述语序（主+谓）：He asked me where the station was.（不是 where was the station）
③ 时态呼应
- 主句现在时→从句任何时态：He says he will go / went / is reading.
- 主句过去时→从句过去相应时态：He said he was reading. / He said he had finished.
- 从句为客观真理时，始终用一般现在时：The teacher told us that the earth travels around the sun.
if/whether 区别
whether可与or not连用，if不可：I don't know whether he will come or not.
whether可用于介词后/不定式前，if不可：It depends on whether he agrees. / I haven't decided whether to go.
宾从三要素：连接词 + 陈述语序 + 时态呼应 | that(陈述)/if-whether(是否)/疑问词(特殊)
🔹 定语从句
定义 在句子中作定语的从句，修饰名词或代词（先行词）
关系代词
关系词指代从句中成分
who人主语/宾语
whom人宾语
which物主语/宾语
that人/物主语/宾语
whose人/物定语（...的）
关系副词
关系词先行词相当于
when时间in/at/on + which
where地点in/at + which
why原因for which
只能用that不用which的情况
① 先行词为不定代词（all, everything, nothing, something, anything, little, much, few）
② 先行词被序数词/最高级修饰（the first, the best）
③ 先行词被the only, the very, the same, the last修饰
④ 先行词既有人又有物
⑤ 主句以who/which开头的疑问句
只能用which不用that的情况
① 非限制性定语从句（逗号隔开）
② 介词+which
限制性 vs 非限制性
限制性：不可去掉，无逗号：I have a brother who works in Beijing.
非限制性：补充说明，有逗号：I have a brother, who works in Beijing.
人用who/that | 物用which/that | 时间when | 地点where | 所属whose | that最万能
🔹 状语从句
时间状语从句
when 当...时（可瞬间可持续）：I was reading when he came in.
while 当...时（持续/进行）：While I was reading, he came in.
as 一边...一边/随着：He sang as he walked. / As time went on, he grew older.
until 直到...：I waited until he came.（一直等到他来）/ I didn't leave until he came.（直到他来才离开）
before/after/as soon as/since/till
时态规则 时间状语从句用一般现在时表将来（"主将从现"）：I will call you when I arrive.
条件状语从句
if 如果 | unless 除非 = if...not
You will pass the exam if you study hard.
You will fail unless you study hard.
时态规则 条件状语从句用一般现在时表将来（"主将从现"）
原因状语从句
because 因为（直接原因，最强）：He didn't come because he was ill.（回答why）
since 既然（已知原因）：Since you are tired, have a rest.
as 由于（附带原因）：As it rained, we stayed home.
目的状语从句
so that 以便 | in order that 为了：He got up early so that he could catch the bus.
结果状语从句
so...that 如此...以至于：He is so young that he can't go to school.
such...that 如此...以至于：It is such an interesting book that I want to read it again.
so vs such so + 形/副 | such + 名（或such + a/an + 形 + 名）
so interesting a book = such an interesting book
让步状语从句
although/though 虽然（不能与but连用）
even though/even if 即使：Even if it rains, I will go.
比较状语从句
as...as 和...一样 | than 比：Tom is as tall as Mike. / He runs faster than I.
"主将从现"原则 时间状语从句和条件状语从句中，主句用一般将来时，从句用一般现在时：I will tell him when he comes back. / If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home.
时间/条件状从："主将从现" | because(原因)/though(让步)/so that(目的)/so...that(结果)
六、句子类型与特殊结构
🔹 句子成分
成分说明例
主语动作的执行者/描述对象Tom runs. / Reading is fun.
谓语动作/状态He runs. / She is a teacher.
宾语动作的承受者I like music. / He gave me a book.
表语系动词后说明主语He is tall. / It looks beautiful.
定语修饰名词a beautiful girl / the book on the desk
状语修饰动词/形/副/句子He runs quickly. / I met him yesterday.
宾补补充说明宾语I made him happy. / They named the baby Tom.
五种基本简单句
① 主 + 谓：He runs.
② 主 + 谓 + 宾：I like English.
③ 主 + 谓 + 间宾 + 直宾：He gave me a book.
④ 主 + 谓 + 宾 + 宾补：I made him happy.
⑤ 主 + 系 + 表：She is beautiful.
5种基本句型：主谓·主谓宾·主谓双宾·主谓宾宾补·主系表
🔹 疑问句
一般疑问句 用yes/no回答：Do you like English? Yes, I do. / Are you a student? No, I'm not.
特殊疑问句 用疑问词提问：What/Who/Whose/Which/When/Where/Why/How（及how many/much/long/often/far/soon/old）
① 疑问词作主语/修饰主语（陈述语序）：Who is your teacher? / Which book is yours?
② 疑问词作其他成分（倒装语序）：What do you like? / Where does he live?
选择疑问句 提供两个以上选项：Do you like tea or coffee? / Which is bigger, the sun or the moon?
反意疑问句 "陈述句+简短疑问"，前肯后否/前否后肯：
He is a teacher, isn't he?（他是老师，是吗？）
He isn't a teacher, is he?（他不是老师，是吗？）
回答反意疑问句 根据事实回答，yes+肯定，no+否定：
— He isn't a teacher, is he? — Yes, he is.（不，他是老师。）/ No, he isn't.（是的，他不是。）
一般疑问：be/do/情态+主语 | 特殊：疑问词+一般疑问（疑问词作主语时陈述语序）
🔹 祈使句 & 感叹句
祈使句 表示命令/请求/建议，省略主语you，动词原形开头：
肯定：Open the door. / Be quiet. / Let's go.
否定：Don't open the door. / Don't be late. / Never give up.
感叹句 表示强烈情感（what修饰名词，how修饰形容词/副词）
What 感叹句
What + a/an + 形容词 + 可数单数 + (主语+谓语)!：What a beautiful girl she is!
What + 形容词 + 可数复数/不可数 + (主语+谓语)!：What beautiful flowers they are! / What bad weather it is!
How 感叹句
How + 形容词/副词 + (主语+谓语)!：How beautiful she is! / How fast he runs!
How + 形容词 + a/an + 可数单数 + (主语+谓语)!：How beautiful a girl she is!
速记口诀 感叹句并不难，what/how是关键；what名(词)来修饰，how形(容词)副(词)跟后面
what + 名词短语 | how + 形容词/副词 | what a beautiful girl = how beautiful a girl
🔹 There be 句型
结构 There be + 名词 + 地点/时间状语（表示"某处有某物"）
现在：There is/are | 过去：There was/were | 将来：There will be / There is going to be
be动词就近原则 如果主语是多个并列名词，be动词与最靠近它的名词保持一致：
There is a pen and two books on the desk.
There are two books and a pen on the desk.
与 have 的区别
There be 表示"某处存在某物"（客观存在），have表示"某人拥有某物"（所属关系）：
There is a book on the desk.（桌上有一本书）
I have a book.（我有一本书）
变形
① There is/are + 名词 + doing（有...正在做）：There is a bird singing in the tree.
② There seems to be / There used to be / There must be：There used to be a river here.
There be + 名 + 地点 | be动词就近原则 | there be(存在) ≠ have(拥有)
🔹 主谓一致
三原则
语法一致 主语单数→谓语单数，主语复数→谓语复数：He likes music. / They like music.
意义一致 主语形式单数但意义复数→谓语复数：The police are looking for him. / The team are having dinner.（队员们）
就近一致 谓语与最靠近的主语一致：
There is a pen and two books. / Either you or he is wrong.
常见特殊情况
① 主语+with/together with/as well as/along with + 名词：谓语与主语一致（不是with后的名词）：Tom, together with his friends, is playing basketball.
② either...or / neither...nor / not only...but also：谓语与靠近的主语一致：Not only Tom but also his parents like music.
③ 不定代词（everyone, someone, nobody, each, either, neither）+ 单数谓语：Everyone likes him.
④ 时间/距离/金钱/度量衡作主语→单数谓语：Ten years is a long time.
⑤ (a) number of：A number of students are... | The number of students is...
语法一致(形) | 意义一致(意) | 就近一致(位) | with/as well as → 前主决定谓语
🔹 直接引语 & 间接引语
直接引语→间接引语的变化原则
人称变化 一随主、二随宾、三不变：
He said, "I like music." → He said that he liked music.
She said to me, "You are right." → She told me that I was right.
时态变化 主句过去时→从句后退一个时态：
一般现在→一般过去 | 现在进行→过去进行 | 现在完成→过去完成 | 一般过去→过去完成 | 一般将来→过去将来 | 情态动词：can→could, may→might, must→must/had to
客观真理/习惯不变：He said that the earth travels around the sun.
时间/地点变化
now→then | today→that day | tonight→that night | yesterday→the day before | tomorrow→the next day | here→there | this→that | come→go
祈使句变化 动词改为ask/tell/order sb to do：
"Sit down," he said. → He told me to sit down.
"Don't move," she said. → She told me not to move.
直引变间引：人称变+时态退+时间改 | 祈使句：tell/ask + sb + to do
🔹 倒装句（常见结构）
初中常见倒装结构
so/neither/nor 倒装 表示"...也一样"：
— I like music. — So do I.（我也是）
— I don't like math. — Neither do I.（我也不）
— He can swim. — So can I.
Here/There 倒装
Here comes the bus.（注意主语是代词时不倒装：Here he comes.）
There goes the bell.
Only + 状语 倒装
Only then did I realize the truth.
Only in this way can we succeed.
So+助/be/情态+主语（肯定）| Neither/Nor+助/be/情态+主语（否定）
