✍️ 初中英语 · 完形填空

解题方法 · 常见考点 · 示例练习 · 中考必备

一、完形填空解题总纲
🔹 题型特点
完形填空（Cloze Test）是中考英语必考题型，通常一篇短文10-15个空，每空4个选项。
考查内容
① 词汇辨析：名词/动词/形容词/副词/介词/连词的选择
② 语法知识：时态/语态/非谓语/从句/固定搭配
③ 上下文逻辑：衔接与连贯、因果/转折/递进/顺序关系
④ 语篇理解：整体把握文章主旨和作者意图
失分原因
① 不看上下文，只看空格所在句就选（最致命）
② 词义辨析不精（形近词/近义词混淆）
③ 固定搭配记忆不清
④ 逻辑关系判断错误
⑤ 文化背景知识欠缺
完形填空 = 词汇 + 语法 + 逻辑 + 语篇理解 | 关键：上下文联动
🔹 解题四步法
第一步：通读全文（2-3分钟）
① 不看选项，快速通读全文，了解文章大意（记叙文→谁+何时+何地+做了何事；说明文→说明对象+特征）
② 遇到空格直接跳过，关注文章整体框架
③ 尤其注意首句和尾句——首句常提示主题，尾句常总结主旨
第二步：初选答案（5-6分钟）
① 逐空分析，先做最有把握的（固定搭配/语法结构/上下文直接提示）
② 拿不准的先标记，第二遍再集中攻克
③ "瞻前顾后"——前文后文都要看
第三步：攻克难题（2-3分钟）
① 联系上下文，寻找线索词
② 排除法：先排除明显不符合逻辑/语法的选项
③ 代入验证：把剩余选项代入空格，看全文是否通顺
第四步：复读检查（1-2分钟）
① 把选好的答案填入短文，从头到尾读一遍
② 检查时态是否一致、逻辑是否通顺、语法是否正确
③ 不要轻易改答案——除非发现明显错误（第一直觉往往更准）
通读→初选→攻克→复检 | 首句定主题，前后找线索，代入验通顺
二、各题型解题技巧
🔹 词汇辨析类
名词辨析
① 单复数是否与上下文一致
② 可数不可数
③ 名词所有格
动词辨析
① 及物/不及物（后是否需要宾语）
② 时态/语态是否与上下文一致
③ 短语动词的固定搭配（look for/after/forward to/up/into/out）
形容词/副词辨析
① 比较级/最高级的标志词（than, of all, in the class）
② 修饰语位置：形容词修饰名词 | 副词修饰动词/形容词/全句
③ 以-ly结尾的不一定是副词：friendly, lovely, lonely, lively 是形容词
近义词辨析（高频）
say/speak/talk/tell：say+内容；speak+语言；talk交谈（talk to/with/about）；tell sb sth/tell stories
look/see/watch/read：look+at（看的过程）；see（看到的结果）；watch（观看动态）；read（读书/报）
bring/take/fetch/carry：bring带来；take带走；fetch去取来；carry搬运
spend/take/cost/pay：spend+时间/金钱+(in) doing/on sth（人作主语）；take+时间（it作主语）；cost+金钱（物作主语）；pay+for（人作主语）
other/another/the other/others/the others：another(另一个，三者以上)；the other(另一个，两者中)；others(其他人/物)；the others(其余全部)
each/every：each强调个体（each of us）；every强调整体（every student）
say+内容/speak+语言/tell+人/watch动态/see结果 | bring来/take去/fetch取
🔹 语法知识类
时态标志词
· 一般现在：often/usually/always/sometimes/every day
· 一般过去：yesterday/last night/...ago/in 2020
· 一般将来：tomorrow/next week/in the future/soon
· 现在进行：now/at the moment/Listen!/Look!
· 现在完成：already/yet/ever/never/just/since/for/recently
· 过去完成：by/before + 过去时间（"过去的过去"）
情态动词推测试题
must（一定）/ can't（不可能）/ may/might（可能）：根据语气强弱选择
介词固定搭配
· 时间介词：at+钟点/on+天/in+月年
· 短语：be good at, be interested in, be afraid of, be proud of, be famous for, be full of, be filled with, be covered with, be known as/for
连词逻辑判断
· 因果：because/so/therefore/thanks to/due to
· 转折：but/however/yet/while/although
· 并列：and/or/not only...but also/either...or/neither...nor
时态看标志词 | 情态看语气 | 介词看固定搭配 | 连词看逻辑关系
🔹 上下文逻辑类
逻辑关系判断
逻辑关系关键词前后内容关系
并列and, also, as well as前后同向
递进even, what's more, besides进一步
转折but, however, yet, while前后相反
因果because, so, therefore, thus前因后果/前果后因
条件if, unless, as long as条件与结果
顺序first, then, next, finally时间或步骤
前后复现线索
① 原词复现：同一词在前文出现 → 选择一样的词或同义词
② 同义复现：前面用了happy，后面可能用glad/cheerful
③ 反义复现：前面用了hot，后面出现cold形成对比
④ 代词指代：空格后的代词指代前文的哪个人/物
特别注意 完形填空的答案往往隐藏在"上下文"中，有时在空格前2-3句、有时在后一句
前后逻辑：转折相反/因果相连/并列同向 | 复现线索：原词/同义/反义/指代

🔹 句型结构汇总
it作形式主语句型
It + is + adj + (for/of sb) + to do sth：It is important for us to learn English.
It + is + adj + that从句：It is clear that he is right.
It takes/took sb + 时间 + to do：It took me two hours to finish the work.
强调句型
It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 剩余部分：It was Tom who helped me.
There be 句型
There is/are + 名词 + doing/地点状语：There is a boy standing there.
比较级句型
as + 原级 + as：Tom is as tall as Mike.
not as/so + 原级 + as：Tom is not as tall as Mike.
比较级 + than：Tom is taller than Mike.
the + 比较级, the + 比较级：The more you read, the more you learn.
so...that / such...that
so + 形/副 + that：He is so young that he can't go to school.
such + 名 + that：He is such a young boy that he can't go to school.
定语从句连接
who(人)/which(物)/that(人/物)/when(时间)/where(地点)/why(原因)
宾语从句语序
连接词 + 主语 + 谓语（陈述语序）：I don't know where he lives.（不是 where does he live）
it is + adj + to do | 比较级+than | so/such...that | 宾从用陈述语序

🔹 示例一 · 记叙文
It was a sunny day. Tom and his friends decided to go to the beach. They 1 very excited. They took their swimsuits and towels. When they arrived, they saw many people 2 in the sea. Tom couldn't wait to join them. He quickly put on his swimsuit and ran into the water. Suddenly, he 3 a cry for help. A boy was drowning! Tom was a good swimmer. He swam to the boy as fast as he could and 4 him to the beach. The boy's parents were very thankful. They said Tom was a 5 boy.

1. A. is    B. was    C. were    D. are
2. A. swim    B. swimming    C. swam    D. to swim
3. A. heard    B. saw    C. felt    D. smelt
4. A. pushed    B. pulled    C. carried    D. brought
5. A. brave    B. lazy    C. shy    D. funny
答案与解析
1. C — 时态题。故事是一般过去时，主语They是复数 → were。
2. B — 非谓语。see sb doing（看到某人正在做某事）。
3. A — 词汇辨析。听到呼救声用heard（a cry for help）。
4. B — 词义辨析。把溺水者"拉"上岸用pulled。push是推，carry是搬运，bring是带来。
5. A — 上下文推断。救人 → 勇敢的 = brave。
🔹 示例二 · 说明文
Do you know how to stay healthy? Here is some 1. First, you should eat a balanced diet. Vegetables and fruits are 2 for your health. Second, exercise is also very important. You should exercise at 3 three times a week. Third, get enough sleep. If you don't get enough sleep, you will feel 4 the next day. Finally, don't 5 too much time playing computer games. It's bad for your eyes.

1. A. advice    B. advices    C. question    D. problem
2. A. bad    B. good    C. harmful    D. well
3. A. last    B. first    C. least    D. most
4. A. happy    B. excited    C. tired    D. relaxed
5. A. take    B. cost    C. pay    D. spend
答案与解析
1. A — 名词不可数。advice是不可数名词，不能加s。这里指"建议"，不是"问题"。
2. B — 上下文。蔬菜水果对健康"好"→good。be good for = 对...有好处。
3. C — 固定短语。at least（至少）。at most（至多），at last（终于）。
4. C — 上下文。睡眠不足→感到"疲劳"→tired。
5. D — 词义辨析。spend time (in) doing（花时间做某事）。take/cost/pay不符合。
🔹 示例三 · 夹叙夹议
Once there was a young man who was very rich but always unhappy. He went to a wise old man and asked, "How can I find happiness?" The old man 1 him to go into the next town and find a family that was truly happy. The young man walked for days. He saw rich families and poor families, but none 2 truly happy. 3, he met a family living in a small house. The father was blind, the mother was sick, but they were all singing and laughing 4. The young man asked, "How can you be so happy 5 you have so little?" The father smiled and said, "We have each other, and that is enough." The young man finally understood: happiness is not about what you have, but about how you feel about what you have.

1. A. asked    B. told    C. said    D. spoke
2. A. is    B. was    C. are    D. were
3. A. At first    B. At last    C. At once    D. At least
4. A. sadly    B. angrily    C. happily    D. nervously
5. A. when    B. because    C. if    D. although
答案与解析
1. B — 固定搭配。told sb to do（让某人做某事）。said不接sb to do；asked请求，不太符合"智者让他去做"的语境。
2. D — 时态与主谓一致。故事过去时，none（没有一个）= not one → 单数也可复数也可，但前文"families"是复数，且语境强调没有一个家庭是真正快乐的。这里用were。
3. B — 逻辑连接。走了很多天"最后"遇到了 → at last。
4. C — 上下文。singing and laughing → 快乐地 = happily。
5. D — 逻辑关系。"你们这么快乐虽然拥有这么少"→ although（尽管）。不是"因为"、"如果"、"当"。

🔹 示例五 · 综合练习

They don't have real feelings or thoughts. 3 robots can do many things faster than humans, they cannot think creatively. 2. A. that    B. what    C. which    D. who

答案与解析
1. A — 转折关系。前句"有人认为机器人会接管世界"与后句"我不这么认为"形成转折 → However。注意however后有逗号。

4. B — 介词后接动名词。instead of + doing（而不是...）。
5. C — 词义辨析。help sb do（帮助某人做某事）。make/let sb do是"让/迫使"，ask sb to do是"要求"。
五、完形填空避坑指南
🔹 常见陷阱与应对
陷阱一：只看空格不看上下文
完形填空最大的忌讳——看到空格就直接凭"语法正确"选，而不看上下文意思。往往语法上多个选项都对，但语义上只有一个正确。
应对 每次看完空格后，至少往前读一句、往后读一句。

陷阱二：被"常识"误导
有些选项符合常识，但不符合文章内容。要根据文章判断，而不是自己的认知。
应对 "文章说啥就是啥"，不要用外部知识代替文章信息。

陷阱三：近义词辨析不精
say/speak/talk/tell, bring/take/fetch/carry, spend/take/cost/pay等高频近义词混淆。
应对 熟记每组近义词的核心区别和典型用法。

陷阱四：固定搭配记不牢
如 look forward to 后接doing而不是do；used to do（过去常常）vs be used to doing（习惯）vs be used to do（被用来）。
应对 平时积累错题本，把常错固定搭配记下来反复背。

陷阱五：时态判断失误
忽略全文时态基调（是过去还是现在），导致选错。
应对 读第一遍时就要判断文章的基本时态，全文保持一致。
